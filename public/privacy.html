Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information REscript collects, how we use it, when we share it, and what controls users have.

Effective date: March 5, 2026

At a glance
  • We collect account, preference, subscription, and operational delivery data.
  • We use data to deliver scripts, operate billing, improve reliability, and provide support.
  • We do not sell personal information for third-party advertising.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to information collected through REscript websites, emails, APIs, and subscription workflows that link to this page.

2. Information we collect

  • Identity and account data: email address and account identifiers.
  • Preference data: language, market, and audience profile selections.
  • Subscription and billing metadata: plan status, Stripe customer and subscription IDs, and payment lifecycle events.
  • Operational data: delivery logs, access token activity, and support records.

3. How we use information

  • Provide and operate REscript services, including daily content delivery and link-based viewing access.
  • Process subscriptions, manage billing state, and handle account administration.
  • Improve performance, security, abuse prevention, and quality control.
  • Respond to support requests and service communications.

4. Legal basis and consent

Where required by applicable law, we process personal data based on contractual necessity, legitimate business interests, legal obligations, and user consent where applicable.

5. Sharing and service providers

We share information only with trusted providers needed to run REscript, such as hosting, email delivery, AI generation, observability, and payment processing providers (including Stripe). We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

6. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries where our providers operate. We use commercially reasonable measures to protect transferred data.

7. Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, maintain account history, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type and legal requirement.

8. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data. No system is completely secure, so users should also protect their credentials and private links.

9. Your choices and rights

  • Update preference settings (market, language, audience mode) from your manage-subscription page.
  • Pause or resume delivery, and manage billing through Stripe for paid subscriptions.
  • Request account support or data-related inquiries by contacting us.

10. Children’s privacy

REscript is intended for professional use and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Policy updates

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version and effective date will always be posted on this page.

12. Contact

For privacy or data requests, contact support@kevv.ai.
