Privacy Policy

This page is the product privacy baseline for REscript. It should be reviewed by counsel before production launch.

What we collect

  • Email address, language preference, market preference, and subscription status.
  • Stripe billing metadata needed to manage paid subscriptions.
  • Operational logs for delivery, billing, and support.

How we use data

  • Deliver daily scripts and manage subscription status.
  • Provide billing support and respond to unsubscribe requests.
  • Improve reliability, fraud prevention, and service monitoring.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to operate REscript, including email delivery, AI generation, hosting, and payment processing.

Your choices

You can pause or resume delivery from the manage-subscription link in every email. Paid customers can also manage billing through Stripe.

Contact

Questions can be sent to support@kevv.ai.
